Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We pride ourselves on a fast response time — typically within 18 minutes during working hours. After hours, we respond first thing the next business day.

Do you offer free site assessments?

Yes! We offer complimentary on-site assessments within Ogun and Lagos State for qualifying projects. This helps us design the most effective solution for your specific needs.

What industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including corporate offices, healthcare facilities, educational institutions, manufacturing plants, retail chains, and residential complexes.

Do you provide after-installation support?

Absolutely. All our installations come with a warranty and we offer maintenance packages to ensure your systems remain optimal. Our support team is available 6 days a week.

Can you handle large enterprise projects?

Yes, JayTech is equipped to handle projects of any scale — from single-office setups to multi-site enterprise deployments across multiple states.

How do I get a detailed quotation?

Simply fill out the contact form on this page with as much detail as possible about your project. Our team will review your requirements and send a comprehensive proposal within 24 hours.
