About JayTech

Who We Are

JayTech Smart Systems Ltd is a forward-thinking ICT solutions company headquartered in Ibafo, Ogun State, Nigeria. We were founded on the belief that every business — regardless of size — deserves access to world-class technology that is reliable, affordable, and built to last.

We specialize in designing, deploying, and maintaining cutting-edge technology infrastructure across industries. From SMEs to large enterprises, our clients trust us to keep their operations connected, secure, and powered — 24/7.

Our Story

Built from Passion. Powered by Purpose.

JayTech Smart Systems was born from a simple frustration: too many Nigerian businesses were left behind by expensive, inaccessible technology providers. Our founder set out to change that — building a company where local businesses could access premium ICT services with the personal attention and care they deserve.

Since our founding, we have grown from a small networking consultancy into a full-spectrum smart systems company, serving clients across multiple states, industries, and sectors — always guided by our core promise: excellence in every connection we make.
