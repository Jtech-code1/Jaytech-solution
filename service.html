Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to enquiries?

We typically respond within 18 minutes during working hours (Mon–Fri 8 AM–6 PM, Sat 9 AM–3 PM). After hours, we follow up first thing the next business day.

Do you offer free site assessments?

Yes! Complimentary on-site assessments are available within Ogun and Lagos State for qualifying projects including solar, CCTV, networking, and cabling.

What industries do you serve?

We serve corporate offices, healthcare, education, manufacturing, retail, and residential sectors across Nigeria with all 8 of our service lines.

Do you provide post-installation support?

All installations include a warranty plus optional maintenance packages. Our support team operates 6 days a week covering solar, CCTV, networking, access control, and more.

Can you handle large enterprise projects?

Absolutely. JayTech handles any scale — single office to multi-site enterprise deployments spanning multiple states, combining networking, CCTV, structured cabling, and access control.

Do you install Starlink for businesses?

Yes. We provide Starlink setup, integration with existing LAN infrastructure, and ongoing ISP support for businesses anywhere in Nigeria needing high-speed satellite connectivity.

How do I get a detailed quotation?

Fill out the contact form above, select your service(s) of interest, and attach any relevant documents. We'll review your requirements and deliver a comprehensive proposal within 24 hours.

Can I bundle multiple services together?

Definitely! Many of our clients combine services — for example, solar + Starlink + CCTV + access control. Bundled projects often attract discounted pricing. Mention it in your message.
